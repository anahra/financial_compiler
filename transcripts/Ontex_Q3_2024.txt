Ontex Q3 2024 Earnings Call Summary (Transcript Unavailable)

Date: October 24, 2024
Participants: Geoffroy Raskin (IR), Gustavo Paz (CEO), Geert Peeters (CFO)

Key Highlights:
- **Revenue:** €468 million. Like-for-like increase of 1.7% year-on-year and 3% sequential increase from Q2.
- **Volume & Mix:** 4.4% increase in volumes/mix, driven by new contracts and robust adult care demand.
- **Pricing:** Sales prices decreased by 2.6% due to lower raw material costs but have stabilized vs Q2.
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** Surged 29% to €56 million. Margin improved to 12.0% (+2.4pp).
- **Cost Transformation:** Delivered €40 million in net savings in the quarter (manufacturing, logistics, purchasing).
- **Debt:** Net debt decreased by €9 million to €579 million. Leverage ratio dropped to 2.4x.
- **Outlook:** Reaffirmed 2024 guidance: Adjusted EBITDA margin ~12%, Free Cash Flow > €20m, Leverage < 2.5x.
- **CEO Commentary:** Gustavo Paz stated the company is "back and may be stronger than ever before."
