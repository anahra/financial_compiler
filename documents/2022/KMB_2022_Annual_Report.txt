[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2022 Annual Report (Form 10-K)]

Filing Date: February 9, 2023
Period Ending: December 31, 2022

## Financial Highlights:
*   **Net Sales:** $20.2 billion (+4% YoY). Unfavorable FX reduced sales by 3%.
*   **Organic Sales:** +7%. Driven by +10% price, +1% mix, offset by -3% volume (elasticity impact).
*   **Operating Profit:** $2.68 billion (Reported). Adjusted Operating Profit decreased 8% to $2.62 billion.
*   **EPS:** $5.72 (Reported), $5.63 (Adjusted).
*   **Input Costs:** Increased by $1.5 billion (pulp, energy, distribution).
*   **Dividends:** 51st consecutive annual increase.

## Strategic Highlights:
*   **Acquisition:** Completed acquisition of majority stake in Thinx Inc. (period underwear).
*   **Divestiture:** Agreed to sell Brazil tissue assets (Neve brand).
*   **Innovation:** Focus on "elevating" categories to premium tiers to combat inflation.
*   **Supply Chain:** Continued challenges with global supply constrains and inflation.

## Key Risks Identified:
*   Significant input cost inflation.
*   Private label trade-down risk due to aggressive pricing actions.
