[Summary of Kimberly-Clark at CAGNY 2022]

Event: Consumer Analyst Group of New York Conference
Date: February 25, 2022

## Key Strategy Update:
*   **Transformation:** Announced improved operating model "Wiring for Growth".
*   **Portfolio:** Confirmed strategy to become a "pure-play personal care and wellness company".
*   **Innovation:** Focus on "Tier Up" strategy - encouraging consumers to trade up to premium tiers (e.g., Huggies Special Delivery).
*   **Digital:** Digital marketing spend now >70% of media mix. E-commerce >25% of sales.

## Financial Goals:
*   Reaffirmed long-term growth algorithm: 1-3% Net Sales, Mid-Single Digit EPS growth.
*   Targeting >$3 billion in gross productivity savings over time.

Note: Key pivot point presentation for the "post-COVID" strategy.
