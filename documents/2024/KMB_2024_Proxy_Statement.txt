[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2024 Proxy Statement]

Filing Date: March 11, 2024
Key Focus: Executive Compensation and Strategic Goals

## Executive Compensation & Performance Linkage:
*   **"Powering Care" Strategy:** Compensation explicitly linked to the successful rollout of the new strategy (Growth, Profitability, Returns).
*   **CEO Compensation:** CEO Mike Hsu total compensation ~$16.4 million. 
    *   Significant portion in Stock Awards ($11M), tying personal wealth to shareholder value.
*   **Pay Ratio:** CEO to Median Employee pay ratio is 305:1.

## Strategic Goals Incentivized:
*   **Organic Net Sales Growth:** A primary metric for short-term incentives.
*   **Adjusted EPS:** Ensuring profitability keeps pace with growth.
*   **Free Cash Flow:** Ensuring the dividend and buybacks are funded.
*   **Strategic Transformation:** Specific non-financial goals related to the organizational restructure (wiring for growth).

Note: This document reveals what metrics management is *paid* to hit, which is arguably the strongest signal of company priority.
