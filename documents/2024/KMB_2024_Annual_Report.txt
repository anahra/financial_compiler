[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2024 Annual Report (Form 10-K)]

Filing Date: February 13, 2025
Period Ending: December 31, 2024

## Financial Highlights:
*   **Net Sales:** $20.1 billion (-1.8% YoY). Decline driven by FX and divestitures.
*   **Organic Sales:** +3.2% (Price and Mix led).
*   **Operating Profit:** $3.21 billion (+36.9%). Adjusted OP +9.4%.
*   **EPS:** $7.55 (Reported), $7.30 (Adjusted).
*   **Gross Margin:** 35.8% (+210 bps). Adjusted GM 36.5%.
*   **Cash Flow:** $3.2 billion from operations.

## Strategic Highlights:
*   **"2024 Transformation Initiative":** Launched to overhaul the operating model and supply chain.
    *   Goal: $3.0 billion in pre-tax savings by 2026.
    *   Impact: Workforce reductions of 4-5%.
    *   Restructuring: Segments reorganized into North America, International Personal Care (IPC), and International Family Care (IFP).
*   **Divestitures:** Sold Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) business for $635 million.
*   **Capital Allocation:** Repurchased $1.0 billion in shares; 52nd annual dividend increase.

## Operational Context:
*   Significant focus on "rewiring" the organization for agility.
*   Strong year for margin recovery despite top-line headwinds from currency.
