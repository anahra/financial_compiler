[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2023 Sustainability Report]

Published: June 2024 (Covering 2023 data)

## Key Milestones:
*   **Water:** Surpassed 2030 Water Footprint target 7 years early.
*   **Climate:** 40.9% reduction in Operational (Scope 1 & 2) GHG emissions. On track for 50% by 2030.
*   **Plastics:** 16.4% reduction in plastics footprint (Target: 50% by 2030). 

## New Ambitions:
*   **100% Natural Forest Free:** Aims to be >50% of the way there by 2030. This is a massive shift for a company reliant on wood pulp (tissue).
*   **Sustainable Products:** Launch of reusable menstrual/incontinence products in key markets (Thinx integration).

## Social Impact:
*   Impacted ~101 million people through social programs (sanitation access, women's empowerment).
*   supply chain: Enhanced human rights due diligence tools.

Note: Sustainability is a material risk and opportunity for KMB given its reliance on natural resources.
