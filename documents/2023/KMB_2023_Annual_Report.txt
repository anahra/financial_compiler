[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2023 Annual Report (Form 10-K)]

Filing Date: February 8, 2024
Period Ending: December 31, 2023

## Financial Highlights:
*   **Net Sales:** $20.4 billion (+1% YoY).
*   **Organic Sales:** +5% (driven by +6% price, offset by -1% mix).
*   **Operating Profit:** $2.34 billion (Reported), $2.96 billion (Adjusted).
*   **EPS:** $5.21 (Reported), $6.57 (Adjusted).
*   **Gross Margin:** 34.4% (+360 bps), driven by $325M in FORCE savings and net revenue realization.

## Operational Highlights:
*   **Personal Care:** +5% organic sales growth.
*   **Restructuring:** Continued execution of the global restructuring program.
*   **Acquisitions/Divestitures:** Completed acquisition of Thinx Inc. and divestiture of Brazil tissue assets.
*   **Sustainability:** On track to halve environmental footprint by 2030.

## Risk Factors (Key Excerpts):
*   Input cost volatility (pulp, polymer, energy).
*   Competitive activity and private label pressure.
*   Geopolitical instability in key markets.
