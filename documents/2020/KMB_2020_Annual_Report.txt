[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2020 Annual Report (Form 10-K)]

Filing Date: February 2021
Period Ending: December 31, 2020

## Financial Highlights:
*   **Net Sales:** $19.1 billion (+4% YoY).
*   **Organic Sales:** +6% (Volume +3%, Price +2%, Mix +1%). Record growth year.
*   **EPS:** $6.87 (Reported), $7.74 (Adjusted) - +12% growth.
*   **Operating Profit:** $3.24 billion.
*   **Cash Flow:** Record cash flow from operations ($3.7 billion).

## Strategic Highlights:
*   **COVID-19 Impact:** Significant tailwind for Consumer Tissue (toilet paper, towels) demand ("pantry loading").
*   **Acquisition:** Acquired Softex Indonesia for ~$1.2 billion (largest acquisition in years).
*   **Share Repurchase:** Authorized new $5 billion buyback program.

## Operational Context:
*   This was the "COVID Peak" year. Manufacturing ran at full capacity to meet surge demand.
*   Strategic pivot to emerging market growth (Softex).
