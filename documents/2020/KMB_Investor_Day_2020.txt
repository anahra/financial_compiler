[Summary of Kimberly-Clark Global Investor Day 2020]

Event: Strategic Update
Date: 2020 (Mid-Year Update)

## Strategic Priorities:
1.  **Accelerate Pioneering Innovation:** Shift from "incremental" to "pioneering" innovation.
2.  **Optimize Margin Structure:** Move beyond just cost-cutting to "margin management" (Revenue Growth Management).
3.  **Wire for Growth:** Organizational restructure to be faster/agile.

## Financials:
*   Targeting consistent mid-single digit Adjusted EPS growth.
*   Top-tier dividend yield.

## Context:
*   Detailed the plan for the "medium term" using the cash windfall from COVID-19 to restructure and invest in the business for the long haul.
