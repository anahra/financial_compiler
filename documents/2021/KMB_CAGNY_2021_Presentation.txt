[Summary of Kimberly-Clark at CAGNY 2021]

Event: Consumer Analyst Group of New York Conference
Date: February 18, 2021

## Key Themes:
*   **Innovation:** Announced 50% increase in innovation pipeline value vs 2019. Contribution of innovation to growth doubled.
*   **Margin Structure:** Focus on "Force for Growth" (cost savings) to fuel brand investment.
*   **Market Position:** Highlighted holding #1 or #2 share in ~80% of markets.
*   **Sustainability:** Launched new 2030 Sustainability Goals (Carbon footprint reduction, Plastics footprint).

Note: Presentation focused on sustaining the gains made during the COVID-19 surge.
