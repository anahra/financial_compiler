[Summary of Kimberly-Clark 2021 Annual Report (Form 10-K)]

Filing Date: February 9, 2022
Period Ending: December 31, 2021

## Financial Highlights:
*   **Net Sales:** $19.4 billion (+2% YoY).
*   **Organic Sales:** -1% (Volume -4%, Price +2%, Mix +1%).
*   **EPS:** $5.35 (Reported), down significantly from prior year due to inflation/volume deleveraging.
*   **Operating Profit:** Decreased due to $1.5 billion in higher input costs.
*   **Cash Flow:** $2.73 billion from operations.

## Strategic Highlights:
*   **Softex Indonesia:** First full year of integration (Checking acquisition from late 2020).
*   **Restructuring:** Charges related to 2018 Global Restructuring Program continued.
*   **Demand Volatility:** Significant "destocking" effect after the COVID-19 demand surge of 2020.
*   **Strategy:** "K-C 2022" plan concluded; focus shifting to "Elevate and Expand" (2022+ strategy).

## Operational Context:
*   Severe supply chain disruptions and inflation began to impact results significantly in H2 2021.
*   Volume declines reflected comparison against the record COVID-19 spike in 2020.
