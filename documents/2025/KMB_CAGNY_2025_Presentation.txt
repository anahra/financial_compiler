[Summary of Kimberly-Clark at CAGNY 2025]

Event: Consumer Analyst Group of New York Conference
Date: February 2025

## Key Strategic Theme: "Powering Care"
*   **Transformation:** The "Powering Care" strategy is the central narrative, focusing on three pillars:
    1.  **Accelerate Pioneering Innovation:** Invest in science/tech to differentiate. Innovation pipeline value up 50% vs 2019.
    2.  **Optimize Margin Structure:** Modernize supply chain, automation. Targeting ~5% gross productivity savings in 2025.
    3.  **Wire for Growth:** New operating model to increase agility.

## Portfolio Optimization:
*   **Focus:** Concentrating on 12 "Powerhouse Brands" (e.g., Huggies, Kleenex, Kotex) which drive >80% of sales.
*   **Exits:** Continuing to exit low-margin businesses like Private Label Diapers and the PPE business.
*   **JV:** Formed JV with Suzano for international tissue to reduce volatility.

## Financial Outlook (2025):
*   **Organic Growth:** Volume + Mix led growth ahead of market.
*   **Profit:** Low-to-mid single-digit Adjusted Operating Profit growth.
*   **Investment:** $2 billion investment in North America manufacturing over 5 years.

Note: This presentation detailed the "next phase" of KMB's evolution under the new operating model.
